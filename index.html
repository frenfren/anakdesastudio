PT Anak Desa Studio

PT Anak Desa Studio is an independent software company based in Indonesia, focused on building simple and useful mobile applications.

Our Apps

Purple Recorder (Screen Recording)

Purple Recorder is a lightweight Android screen recording app designed for everyday users.

  • Record screen in high quality.
  • No audio from other apps (Android privacy policy).
  • Simple recording list with share and delete options.
  • Small banner ads at the bottom of the recordings list.

Ngorder (Sales App)

Ngorder is a simple sales and ordering app to help small businesses record customer orders and track daily sales.

  • Customer and product list management.
  • Quick order entry for sales teams.
  • Basic daily and monthly sales summaries.

Alfabet Fun (Educational Game)

Alfabet Fun is an educational game that helps children learn letters and sounds in a fun and interactive way.

  • Interactive alphabet lessons with sounds.
  • Simple mini games for early learners.
  • Designed for children with a focus on safe content.
